🧐 Why this website?

Hi there! I’m Malou, also known as Unknown Entity 1806 on the web. I’m a digital artist and web enthusiast who loves creating my own designs. In reaction to social media platforms ruining my online experience, I decided to build my own little corner of the internet where I can share my art and archive my lists and share my freebies with the world 💜
