What is it with Unknown Entity 1806? 🤔

Unknown Entity 1806 (it) was created as I searched for a new online identity in 2024. It is not just an original character, but a persona I created to represent myself online. I used to be known online as Studio Louma. However I struggled with my creativity, as well as just life in general at the time. I felt restricted in what I could create with my online presence. So I wanted a new one. Coincidentally I received my autism diagnosis around the same time. This inspired the creation of my new persona as a weird little alien/robot that did not understand themselves, nor was understood by others; who felt like they were just a number in the world. It has antennas to signify its sensitivity to the world around.
