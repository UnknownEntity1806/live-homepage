Updates: A time for relaxation

May 4, 2026

Hello 👋

It is time for me to give you another monthly update. Although the previous one was not really that long ago.

The weather here in Spain has not been great the past few weeks. Which means that the plans I had to spend outside have not come to fruition.
Instead I have spend more time behind the computer and playing around with HUGO. I have learned alot. Which has also motivated me to update the theme for this website to version 3.1: lilac-code! 💜

Hopefully, this layout will satisfy me for a longer period than the previous version. 👼 As a solution for that I have thought that I can make layouts as freebies. This way I can still continue to implement the new stuff I learn and satisfy my need to create, without having to overhaul my entire website each time.
The first layout theme is already available as a freebie! 👏
It’s called springtime and you can find it here.

About the new version

I myself really like this new version, lilac-code, as I have named it. It’s a bit more minimalistic (for me) and more calm. It’s also more close to what I actually enjoy in terms of aesthetics. The previous version had a lot of harsh colours and pink in it. Whilst I do not even really like pink!

what to expect

The title of this newsletter is A Time for Relaxation, and that is what you can expect from me in the coming weeks. As I said last month, we are entering our lasts weeks here in Spain. And there is still a lot to be done in terms of errands. And anyone that knows Spain, knows that things move slowly here. So I do not expect to be able to do much in terms of creative projects in the remaining time here. I will be going back to the Netherlands with my father in the camper in June. And June itself is already fully booked with lots of appointments. So hopefully in July I will really have time to enjoy creating again. Until then,

Huggies, Malou
