Updates for March 2026

March 14, 2026

First, I finished the Pokemon illustration featuring my OC Chloe as a Pokemon trainer. It was fun thinking about which Pokemon she would have on her team and how to include little Pokemon details in her outfit. You can see the full illustration here. At the same time, I have discovered Pokemon Go. And it has really helped me get out more and explore the village.

Second, I have finished rewatching all seasons of Stranger Things. I really like the 80s vibes in the show. And it has definitely ignited my love for Dungeons and Dragons. But now I am left a bit empty and I do not know what to watch next… Any recommendations are welcome!

Third, I received my Kickstarter package from Grandma Keiko. It’s a book with such lovely details. A true delight to look at. I also got a colouring book, so that will be a fun activity to do the coming weeks.

Lastly, I am in a bit of a rut when it comes to drawing. I have quite a few WIPs I could finish. But somehow each day is a bit busy and I never get to sit down and really finish any illustrations. The same goes for developing my remaining pages on this website. I hope to get to finish those projects in April.
My brother and nephew are coming to visit soon, and then I will really not have time to spend behind the PC … Which ofcourse, it is always a good thing when “real life” gets in the way of hobbies. But I do hope to get to finish some of my projects in the coming month, so that I can share them with you here on the website.
